David Bowie's Achievements

David Bowie wrote some great songs, but he did so much more!

  • Embraced and developed new musical genres: Glam rock, Industrial
  • Dabbled in Broadway and Films: Bowie graced the films "The Man Who Fell to Earth" and "Just a Gigolo" and starred in the Broadway play "The Elephant Man"
  • Was an eclectic collaborator: worked with Madonna, Queen, Brian Eno, Pet shop Boys, Trent Reznor, and Bing Crosby!
  • Created interesting and colorful characters: Ziggy Stardust, Major Tom.
  • Started his own Internet Service Provider: Yes, BowieNet was a thing!
